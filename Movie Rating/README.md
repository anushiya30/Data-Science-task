How It Works

. Load and clean the dataset

. Perform data exploration and visualization:

. Distribution of ratings

. Popular genres and directors

. Trends over years

. Calculate average ratings based on attributes (genre, director, etc.)

. Predict the rating of a new movie using weighted averages or rules

. Display predicted ratings along with visual insights

Technologies Used

. Python 3

. Pandas (data handling)

. NumPy (numerical calculations)

. Matplotlib / Seaborn (visualization)

Project Insights

. Ratings are influenced by genre, director, actors, and release year.

. Simple rule-based predictions can give fast and interpretable results.
