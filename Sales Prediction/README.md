Sales Prediction (Based on Advertising Spend)

Overview
. This project predicts product sales using advertising spend data across TV, Newspaper, and Radio.
. The focus is on data analysis, visualization, and simple statistical predictions, without using machine learning.

Features

. Analyze how advertising spend affects sales.

. Predict sales using averages, correlations, and trends

. Visualize the relationships between ad spend and sales.

. Scatter plots of sales vs each advertising channel

. Trend lines to estimate impact of ads

. Identify the most effective advertising channel for sale

How It Works

. Load and clean the dataset

. Perform data exploration

. Summary statistics

. Correlations between ad spend and sales

. Scatter plots: TV vs Sales, Newspaper vs Sales, Radio vs Sales

. Predict sales using simple statistical methods

Average sales per ad spend range

Trend-based estimation

Display results and visual insights
